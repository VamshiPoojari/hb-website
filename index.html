About Himalaya Bhawan

Himalaya Bhawan is one of the newest girls hostels of IIT Roorkee, designed to offer a modern, comfortable, and inclusive living environment for students. Inspired by the strength and resilience of the Himalayas, the Bhawan reflects a blend of contemporary infrastructure and community-oriented spaces.

Established in 2023, Himalaya Bhawan houses students across multiple floors and is equipped with essential facilities including a dining mess, canteen, guest rooms, common recreational areas, laundry facilities, and high-speed wired internet connectivity. With lift access and eco-friendly design elements, the Bhawan focuses on convenience, sustainability, and student well-being.

Security

Himalaya Bhawan prioritizes the safety and security of all residents with comprehensive 24/7 surveillance and security personnel.

Complete camera coverage across all hostel premises including entrances, corridors, common areas, and outdoor spaces for round-the-clock monitoring.

Security Guards

3 trained security guards providing 24/7 protection to the hostel, ensuring safety of residents at all times.

Entry Management
